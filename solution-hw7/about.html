About

Welcome to my Scarecrow Field! Dive into a world where you create your very own scarecrow to help me guard my field. Here’s how to get started:

Design Your Scarecrow: Click on “Build Your Own” to design a scarecrow.

Deploy Your Creation: Once you’re satisfied with your design, hit “Confirm,” then click anywhere in the field to place your scarecrow and watch it come to life!
